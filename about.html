About Timelly

Empowering teams with innovative time tracking solutions for enhanced productivity and collaboration.

Our Story

Timelly was born from the need to simplify time tracking for modern collaborative teams. We recognised that traditional time tracking methods were outdated and didn't meet the needs of today's dynamic work environments.

Founded by eSense Software Solutions, Timelly combines cutting-edge technology with user-centric design to create a time tracking solution that teams actually want to use.

Our Mission

To revolutionise how teams track, manage, and optimise their time, enabling better productivity, transparency, and work-life balance in the modern workplace.

Our Vision

To become the leading time tracking platform that empowers organisations worldwide to make data-driven decisions about their most valuable resource: time.

Innovation

We continuously evolve our platform with cutting-edge features that anticipate and meet the changing needs of modern teams.

Simplicity

We believe powerful tools should be easy to use. Our intuitive design ensures teams can focus on their work, not learning software.

Security

Your data security is our priority. We implement industry-leading security measures and comply with international standards.

Transparency

We provide clear insights into time usage, helping teams make informed decisions and improve their productivity.

Collaboration

We foster teamwork by providing tools that enhance communication and coordination across all project stakeholders.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from product development to customer support and user experience.

Our Team

Timelly is developed by a passionate team of engineers, designers, and productivity experts at eSense Software Solutions. We're dedicated to creating tools that make work more efficient and enjoyable.

Based in Saudi Arabia, we understand the unique challenges faced by businesses in the region and globally. Our diverse team brings together expertise in software development, user experience design, and business operations.
